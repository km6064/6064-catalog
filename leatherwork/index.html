Fine Leathergoods

Every piece is entirely hand-stitched. Saddle stitching passes thread through both sides of the leather, creating a locked stitch more durable and repairable than machine construction.

Materials

Full grain leathers sourced from historic European tanneries. French goatskin linings. Waxed linen thread.

Cut edges are creased, sanded smooth and painted by hand. The edge paint is sanded and reapplied as many as five times, before polishing perfectly smooth with heat.

Made with intent, restraint, and a focus on longevity.
Designed for use, not for replacement.
