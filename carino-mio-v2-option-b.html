Design philosophy

Construction

Monolithic precision

A single CNC-machined aluminium assembly houses the ribbon motor, transformer, and output XLR. No compromise. No unnecessary parts.

The ridged nylon body provides functional alignment of the internal assembly and visual reference for stereo positioning.

Considered
choices

Every material selected for longevity, repairability, and acoustic performance.

PA12 Nylon

Body and hood SLS printed with shot-peen polish. Chosen for durability and precise tolerance matching.

Nextel Suede

Premium matte finish with slight texture. Feels as good as it looks.

Lundahl Transformer

Paired with N42 Neodymium magnets for efficiency, clarity, and warmth.

Aluminium Assembly

CNC machined motor mount. Houses everything in a single serviceable unit.

Saati Mesh

High-performance fabric bonded to aluminium. Protection without acoustic compromise.

Neutrik XLR

Rear-exiting for freestanding use or easy rigging.

Craft

The ribbon

38mm × 5mm, corrugated from 1.8 micron aluminium foil. The geometry, magnets, and transformer were developed in parallel—each element tuned to complement the others.

The final implementation was professionally installed and tuned to ensure optimal tension and frequency response.
